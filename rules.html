IPL Fantasy League Rules

  1. Each team should consist of 11 players, including a captain and a vice-captain.
  2. Each player will be assigned a certain value (salary cap) based on their performance in the previous IPL season.
  3. The total value of the players in a team cannot exceed the set salary cap.
  4. Points will be awarded to each player based on their performance in each match, including runs scored, wickets taken, catches, run-outs, etc.
  5. Points will also be deducted for negative actions such as giving away runs, dropped catches, etc.
  6. Points will be multiplied by the captain and vice-captain's multiplier, which is set at the beginning of the season.
  7. Trades and transfers will be allowed during the season, with a limit on the number of trades per team.
  8. Prizes will be awarded to the top-performing teams at the end of the season.
